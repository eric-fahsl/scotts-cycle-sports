Hello and welcome to Scott’s Cycle and Sports!  We started in 1993 in Hermiston Oregon by owner Scott Cimmiyotti.  Currently Scott’s Cycle and Sports employs ten passionate bicycle enthusiasts in two locations and continues to be a family business.

Whether you want to get out on the road, singletrack, or compete in triathlons, we have the gear for you! We carry great bikes from Giant, Raleigh, Felt, Orbea and numerous accessories.  Check out our products page for a full list of all of our brands and accessories.
